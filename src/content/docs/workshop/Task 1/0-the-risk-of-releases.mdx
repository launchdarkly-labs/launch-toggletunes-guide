## Welcome to ToggleTunes!

You recently joined the ToggleTunes team as a product manager. ToggleTunes is DJ Toggle's latest creation, a music app designed for the most loyal of fans. The team recently adopted LaunchDarkly to help them accelerate the way they develop, ship and release software.

## The Challenge
Help the ToggleTunes team release new versions and features of their app with speed.

- Create feature flags in LaunchDarkly
- Migrate our backend database
- Leverage segments and targeting to release features to specific users
- Ship a top-tier experience, with speed.
